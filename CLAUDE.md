# AKIRART CAFE STUDIO — Bangkok, Pom Prap Sattru Phai

A retro 80s/90s-office single-page tribute to AKIRART — Kendo Naken & New Athinan's nostalgic cafe + film-development studio tucked above an old Chinatown shophouse.

## Brand brief — verified

- **Name:** AKIRART CAFE STUDIO. IG handle: `@akirart.coffee`
- **Founders:** Kendo Naken & New Athinan (best friends, share a love for film and media) — per TheSmartLocal Dec 2019
- **Origin of name:** "Akirart" was the name Kendo's father came up with for his 90s production company *Kamarart*. He never used it because he worried it would be hard to pronounce. Kendo adopted it in his dad's honour.
- **Opened:** February 2019. Original location was 21 Anuson 1 Alley, Plubplachai (3rd floor above the old Kamarart office). Moved to **138 Soi Pradu, Pom Prap Sattru Phai, Bangkok 10100** (per Bangkok Foodie 2020 + Home Journal 2026).
- **Hours:** Wed–Mon 10:00–18:00 · CLOSED Tuesdays (per Kirby's brief + IG).
- **Atmosphere:** Walking into Akirart "feels like going into a time capsule." Boxy Macintosh computers, beige typewriters, landline phones, printers, floppy disks-as-coasters, steel drawers, books from the 80s/90s, vintage office furniture. The upper area doubles as the waiting room for the film-development service.
- **Film service:** A real film-processing + scanning service on site, with a workspace behind a glass partition labelled "Film Photography Service Processing & Scanning."
- **Photo booth:** An office-themed photo booth on site.
