      ▸ № 02 · README.txt
      
Named for Kendo's dad's 90s production house.

    

    

      
Kendo Naken's father ran a Bangkok production house in the 90s. He nearly named it Akirart but worried it'd be too hard to pronounce — he kept the company as Kamarart instead. Twenty-something years later, his son and a friend, New Athinan, took over the old office on the third floor of an alley shophouse in Plubplachai. They left the floppy disks where they were. They added a coffee machine.

      

        "Walking into Akirart felt like going into a time capsule that brought me back to the era of 'chubby' computers and floppy disks."
        — TheSmartLocal · Eddie Jirayu · Dec 2019
      

      
The cafe opened in February 2019. A year later they moved to a bigger space at 138 Soi Pradu, in Pom Prap Sattru Phai, the corner of Chinatown where Bangkok's printing-house alleys used to live. The vintage Macs migrated with them. So did the steel filing cabinets, the typewriters, the photo booth, and a glass-walled bay labelled Film Photography Service Processing & Scanning — a real on-site film lab that develops 35mm and 120 negatives while you finish your Passion Fruit Espresso.

      
The drinks are good. The dad's name is in the door. The floppy disks come out as coasters under your cup.

        ▸ № 05 · FILM-LAB.SYS
        
A real film-processing & scanning lab.

      

      
The upstairs glass-walled bay is the actual film lab — Akirart processes 35mm and 120 negatives, hands the scans back as files, and exhibits the prints on the wall.

    


    


      

        
FILM.LAB · SERVICE.MENU

        

          

            
Formats accepted

            
35mm colour negative · 35mm B&W · 120 medium format. Drop-off at the bar.


            
Service

            
Processing only · Processing + Scanning · Scanning only. Scanned files delivered via Google Drive link.


            
Turnaround

            
Typically 3–5 days · longer during festivals and weekends.


            
Drop-off hours

            
Wed–Mon 10:00 – 17:30 · Closed Tuesdays · DM @akirart.coffee first if you're bringing multiple rolls.


            
Price guide

            
Ask the bar — varies by film stock and scan resolution. Cheaper than a Bangkok chain lab, slower than mail-order.
